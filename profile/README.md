# Tech Incroft Solutions 🚀  

> Building scalable, cloud-native products for modern businesses

## About Us

We are a product-focused technology company crafting **full‑stack web platforms**, multi‑tenant SaaS solutions, and high‑performance APIs for startups and enterprises.  
Our team blends solid engineering practices with a strong sense of product thinking, ensuring every feature we ship creates real business value.

## 🌟 What We Do

- **SaaS & Platforms** – Multi-tenant apps, subscriptions, metered billing
- **Full‑Stack Development** – From domain modeling to production rollout
- **APIs & Integrations** – GraphQL/REST, payment gateways, third‑party systems
- **Cloud & DevOps** – Secure, observable, automated deployments
- **AI & Automation** – Workflow automation, LLM-assisted tooling
